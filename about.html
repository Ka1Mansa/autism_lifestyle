WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT US

Autism being a life-long disorder, it is up to us as a society to embrace it in order to promote inclusion in our society

Our children struggle with communication, socialization, imagination and sensory perception. They feel lost in our world, and the big question is ....... what are we doing to bridge this gap?

Here at Autism Lifestyle, we are determined and dedicated to encourage parents of children living with Autism, to be fully equipped with both knowledge and skills, so that you can walk your child through this journey of life with Autism.

Join us and let us embark on this challenging yet wonderful life together.

Mission

Enhance lives of individuals on the Austism spectrum and those close to them through awareness and education.

Vision

We envision a world that embraces neurodiversity and celebrates the unique strngths and perspectives of individuals living with Autism.
